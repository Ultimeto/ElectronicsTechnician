Electronic Engineering Technicians

Electronic engineering technicians are involved in many aspects of design, development, testing and manufacturing. Electronic Engineering Technicians solve problems that may appear in areas of research, manufacturing, sales, construction, inspection and maintenance. Their work is specialized in applications while using learned principles of mathematics, science and engineering.

Their work includes:

  • Equipment Building
  • Creating prototype versions of designs
  • Collecting Data
  • Conducting Tests
  • Inspecting Products and Quality Control
