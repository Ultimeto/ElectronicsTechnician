At minimum, a high school education is required to be an electronics technician. But an Associate's degree, apprenticeship at a vocational school or certificate program is recommended for this type of job. Some technicians may be certified by different organizations depending on the type of work they intend to do. Organizations of such organizations include: Electronics Technicians Association and the International Society of Certified Electronics Technicians.
